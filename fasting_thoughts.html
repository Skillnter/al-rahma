Omani woman

Since the beginning of the blessed renaissance, the Omani woman has enjoyed great care and care, and a distinguished honor from His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Saeed Al-Muazzam - may God protect and preserve him - as it has opened before her full opportunities for education in all its stages and levels, work in various fields and participation in the national building process, and it has embodied This is clearly evident since His Majesty took office in 1970 through the civilization vision of the builder of modern Oman renaissance, which is based on full confidence in the capabilities of the Omani citizen on the one hand and working to make the most of the Omani human resources on the other.

His Majesty - may God bless him and preserve him - indicated at the beginning of his assumption of government the need of the country for all elements of society without discrimination, in his first speech to His Majesty on 7/27/1970 AD on the occasion of the arrival of His Majesty to Muscat for the first time where he said “The government and the people are like one body if no member From him, with his duty, the rest of the parts of that body were broken. ”Because of the importance of this sentence, His Majesty repeated it in his speech on August 9, 1970 once again.

So during the course of the march, the Omani woman was able to prove her scientific capabilities and practical competence to demonstrate her worth by the trust granted to her by His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Saeed Al-Muazzam - may God protect and preserve him - and the Omani society. The steps taken by Omani women in the past years and the achievements achieved are the greatest evidence However, the Omani woman is a source of confidence for what she was granted, and she became like a bee, not only absorbing the nectar of one flower, but she always looked for the new and beneficial that enriches her world and builds her community and her family, so she worked at times as a volunteer and another as a complement to the role of the man, so she formed a stable entity for herself. Omani women have a vital role that represents half of society in the national development process, not only through participation in work and effort in this or that field, but also through the vital social role that Omani women play as a mother and housewife in preparing Omani generations, cultivating authentic Omani values ​​and traditions and contributing to them. What remains for Omani women remains a source of pride for all Omanis, as it has received Sultan Qaboos’s interest in making the seventeenth of October of every year a day for Omani women.

Omani women and the development process

The participation of Omani women in the path of national construction derives and draws its roots from the historical and civilizational heritage of the Omani society, which witnessed during its historical periods a presence of Omani women in all fields, and the emergence of the Omani Renaissance that was led by His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Saeed Al-Muazzam - may God protect and preserve him - in 1970 AD. Omani women participate in the development process by redrawing their national roles alongside their male brother to achieve the goal and goal of development.

Hence the assertion that the Omani woman responded to the call of His Majesty - may God bless and preserve him - for her to participate actively in national development by virtue of her primary role in the family and society and then in the national economy. So the Omani women managed during the short development process to achieve leaps in the parliamentary life and decision-making positions. As the empowerment of women and their full participation in the decision-making process and attainment of positions of authority are essential for achieving equality and development, as the right of women to equality in all fields is a natural right by virtue of their existence as an active and influential human being in life. This is evident as Omani women hold senior leadership positions at the levels as minister, deputy, ambassador, second public prosecutor and member of the State Council, Businessmen Council, Municipal Council, and the Oman Chamber of Industry and Board of Directors in addition to many senior administrative positions (general manager - director of management - consultant - expert ).
